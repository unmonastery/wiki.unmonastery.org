===== Description (with anecdote) =====
---------

A rich territory for The Book of Mistakes, - expectations change continuously, so it is really important to document them. Stakeholders' expectations need to be respected, but need to change, too and that can only happen through clear and continuous communication with them. Individual expectations go through radical transformation as people settle into unMonastery existence. The Protocol outlines the expectations of the unMonastery community from each of its members. Is it possible to formulate the expectations of the unMonastery as a community, with regard to their hosting community?
