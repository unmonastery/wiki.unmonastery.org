===== Description (with anecdote) =====
---------

Making consensus as the general decision-making method works great for setting up baselines and general rules of operation which affect everyone. However, taking leadership and allow others to join is a more straightforward path to actually getting things done. Acknowledge that results do not always correlate to work.
