====== Excommunication -- The Fallacy of the E-post ======


Talking, or analog conversation, isn’t the be all and end all; listening isn’t a universally distributed  skill; nor are everyone’s memory banks equally solvent.  However, the good old one-to-one with built in emoticons that encompass a vast range of hidden and double meaning still offer some advantage.  Firstly, one can be reasonably certain that the other person is in the room; the send button is automatically activated, your file is opened.
